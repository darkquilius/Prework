Cage the Elephant Rules!!!

Bio:

Formed in 2006, this American rock band climbed to success in 2008 with their breakout self-titled album. Their first breakout song, "Ain't No Rest for the Wicked" was featured in movies and video games such as the borderlands series. The band has since released five albums and is currently on tour with Beck. Their most recent album, "Social Cues," has been grammy nominated for Best Rock Album.

Members:

Matt Shultz: Lead Vocals, Rythm Guitar Brad Schult: Rythm Guitar, Keyboards Daniel Tichenor: Bass, Backing Vocals Jared Champion: Drums, Percussion
